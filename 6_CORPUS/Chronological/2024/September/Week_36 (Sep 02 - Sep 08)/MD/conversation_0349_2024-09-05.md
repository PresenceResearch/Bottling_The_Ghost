**Shortwave Email Review and Alternatives**

**Shortwave** is an AI-powered email client designed to streamline inbox management, enhance productivity, and provide advanced features like AI-powered summarization and smart scheduling. However, one limitation is that it only works with Gmail, which might be a drawback for users seeking broader compatibility across different email services.

If you're considering alternatives to Shortwave, here are some options:

1. **Canary Mail**: A user-friendly, AI-enabled email client that supports multiple platforms (MacOS, Windows, Android, and iOS) and various email services like Gmail, Yahoo, Office365, and iCloud. It offers unique features like an AI email generator called Inbox CoPilot, bulk cleaning, and a clean interface. Canary Mail has both a free version and a premium version priced at $49 annually, offering additional features.

2. **Thunderbird**: A well-established, open-source email client available for Mac, Windows, and Linux. It offers robust organizational tools, including folders, tags, automation rules, and Smart Folders. Thunderbird is a great free alternative if you're looking for extensive customization and control, although it lacks some modern design elements and integration features.

3. **Microsoft Outlook**: A comprehensive email client that integrates seamlessly with other Microsoft applications like Teams and OneNote. It offers features like a calendar, task management, and powerful organizational tools. Outlook is available as part of the Microsoft 365 subscription at $69.99 annually or as a standalone purchase for $139.99.

4. **eM Client**: A versatile email client that supports a wide range of features, including calendars, live chat, to-do lists, and more. It is designed with a sleek interface and offers both a free version and a premium version priced at $49.95, with an option for lifetime upgrades.

5. **Airmail**: A third-party email client that supports various platforms (Google, Outlook, Yahoo, Exchange, iCloud, and IMAP) and integrates with other apps like OneDrive, Dropbox, and Trello. It offers customizable features like snoozing emails, turning them into PDFs, and blocking senders. Airmail has a free version, with the Airmail Pro plan available for $29.99 annually【6†source】【7†source】.

Each alternative provides a unique set of features, so the best choice will depend on your specific needs, such as platform compatibility, desired features, and budget.
