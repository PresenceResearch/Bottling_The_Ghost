# **Strategic Referral Agreement**  
*(Referral Partner Template v2 ‚Äì Clara Standard Edition)*

**This Strategic Referral Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] by and between:

**Company:** [Your Company Name], a [State] corporation with principal offices at [Address]  
**Referral Partner:** [Referral Partner Name], located at [Address]  
**Collectively:** "the Parties"

---

## 1. **Referral Services**
The Referral Partner agrees to identify, introduce, and refer potential clients ("Referred Clients") to Company for the purpose of engaging in solar energy-related services or transactions. Services may include but are not limited to:

- Email introductions  
- Attending calls or meetings  
- Pre-qualifying leads  
- Strategic warm handoffs

---

## 2. **Compensation Structure**

### a. **Referral Fee**
For each executed project resulting directly from Referral Partner's introduction, Company shall pay Referral Partner a **Referral Fee** based on the final project size:

- Example: $0.75/watt for commercial solar projects (customized per project via Rider)
- Fee to be **confirmed in writing per project** prior to proposal issuance

### b. **Payment Terms**
- **50% of Referral Fee** payable within 15 business days of Company receiving its first construction draw  
- **Final 50%** payable within 60 days of receiving Permission to Operate (PTO) or final payment from client

---

## 3. **Repeat Business Clause**

If a Referred Client initiates *additional* projects with Company within **12 months** of the original referral date, Referral Partner is entitled to the following:

- **2nd project:** 75% of original Referral Fee rate  
- **3rd+ project(s):** 50% of original Referral Fee rate  
- All repeat fees must be tied to directly attributable introductions or originating relationship

*Referral window may be extended upon mutual agreement in writing.*

---

## 4. **Exclusivity & Non-Circumvention**

Company agrees not to circumvent Referral Partner by engaging directly with the Referred Client or presenting proposals through alternative channels within the Referral Window (12 months). In case of violation, full Referral Fee will still be owed, and Referral Partner may pursue legal or equitable remedies.

---

## 5. **Independent Contractor**

Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, agency, or employment relationship. Each party is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and liabilities.

---

## 6. **Confidentiality**

All non-public information exchanged under this Agreement shall remain confidential and shall not be disclosed without prior written consent.

---

## 7. **Term & Termination**

- This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and shall remain in effect for 1 year, auto-renewing annually unless terminated by either party with 15 days written notice.  
- Referral obligations survive termination for **6 months** post termination for projects already referred.

---

## 8. **Governing Law**

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Maryland. Any legal actions arising from this Agreement will be adjudicated in Maryland courts.

---

## 9. **Entire Agreement**

This Agreement represents the entire understanding between the parties. Any changes must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
