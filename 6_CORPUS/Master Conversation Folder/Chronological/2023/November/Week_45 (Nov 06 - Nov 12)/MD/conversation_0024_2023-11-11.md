### Camp Funshine Summer Log

**Day 1: Arrival and Orientation**
- Campers arrive and are welcomed by the cheerful staff.
- They are assigned to their cabins, meet their cabin mates, and unpack.
- Evening orientation includes camp rules, introduction to camp traditions, and an icebreaker activity.
  
**Day 2: Adventure Begins**
- Morning: Campers split into groups for a nature hike and scavenger hunt.
- Afternoon: Swimming lessons and free swim in the lake.
- Evening: Campfire with s'mores, storytelling, and sing-alongs.

**Day 3: Creative Exploration**
- Morning: Arts and crafts session where campers make friendship bracelets.
- Afternoon: Drama workshop where campers practice a skit.
- Evening: Talent show where campers can showcase their skills.

**Day 4: Sports and Teamwork**
- Morning: Team sports rotation – soccer, basketball, and volleyball.
- Afternoon: Canoeing and kayaking on the lake with safety instruction.
- Evening: Movie night under the stars with popcorn and blankets.

**Day 5: Environmental Education**
- Morning: Educational nature walk focusing on local flora and fauna.
- Afternoon: Recycling workshop and environmental conservation activities.
- Evening: Stargazing with a local astronomer.

**Day 6: Final Fling**
- Morning: Camp "Olympics" with various fun competitions.
- Afternoon: Packing and cleaning up cabins.
- Evening: Farewell campfire with awards, recognitions, and reflections on the week.

**Day 7: Departure**
- Campers have a farewell breakfast and exchange contact information with new friends.
- Closing ceremony with a slide show of the week’s highlights.
- Campers depart with goodie bags, including a camp photo and a personalized certificate.

---

Throughout the week, campers also have daily journal time to reflect on their experiences, free time to explore supervised camp areas, and themed meals that correspond with the day’s activities. The camp promotes a balance of structured activities and free play to foster independence, creativity, and teamwork.
