For translation needs, especially if you're looking for something that integrates smoothly with various platforms, here are a few popular tools and extensions:

1. **Google Translate:**
   - **Extension:** The Google Translate browser extension allows you to translate entire web pages or selected text by simply clicking on the extension icon. It's available for Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers.
   - **App:** The Google Translate app is available on both Android and iOS, and it offers features like offline translation, camera translation (for signs, menus, etc.), and handwriting input.

2. **DeepL Translator:**
   - **Extension:** DeepL offers a browser extension that provides high-quality translations, particularly for European languages, but it works well with Japanese too.
   - **App:** DeepL has a desktop app for Windows and macOS that can translate text from any application using a shortcut. It's known for more natural-sounding translations.

3. **Microsoft Translator:**
   - **App:** Available on both Android and iOS, Microsoft Translator supports conversation translation, which is useful for real-time communication. It also offers text, voice, and camera translations.

4. **Mate Translate:**
   - **Extension:** Mate Translate is a powerful browser extension that integrates with Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. It allows for quick translations of selected text and offers phrasebook capabilities.
   - **App:** Mate Translate also has an app for macOS that provides system-wide translations.

5. **Papago:**
   - **App:** Papago is a translation app developed by Naver (a popular search engine in Korea) and is available on Android and iOS. It specializes in Asian languages and offers features like image and conversation translation.

6. **Lingvanex Translator:**
   - **Extension:** Lingvanex offers a browser extension that supports over 100 languages. It’s useful for translating entire web pages or selected text.
   - **App:** Lingvanex has a mobile app and desktop software that offer offline translation, voice translation, and even translation of files like PDFs.

If you're looking for an extension or app that integrates well across various platforms, **Google Translate** is often the most versatile and widely used, especially for quick and general translations. However, if you prioritize translation quality, especially for complex or nuanced text, **DeepL** is highly recommended. 

For Japanese translations specifically, **Google Translate** and **DeepL** are both strong options, with **DeepL** often providing more contextually accurate translations.
