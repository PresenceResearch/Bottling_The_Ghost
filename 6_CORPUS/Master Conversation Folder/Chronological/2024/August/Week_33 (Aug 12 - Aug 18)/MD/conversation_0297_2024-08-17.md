Certainly! Here are a few sentences using the word "good" (良い - *Ii*):

1. **これは良い本です。**  
   *Kore wa ii hon desu.*  
   This is a good book.

2. **あなたのアイデアはとても良いです。**  
   *Anata no aidea wa totemo ii desu.*  
   Your idea is very good.

3. **天気が良いので、散歩に行きましょう。**  
   *Tenki ga ii node, sanpo ni ikimashō.*  
   Since the weather is good, let's go for a walk.

4. **その映画は良い評価を受けています。**  
   *Sono eiga wa ii hyōka o uketeimasu.*  
   That movie has received good reviews.

5. **彼は良い先生です。**  
   *Kare wa ii sensei desu.*  
   He is a good teacher.
