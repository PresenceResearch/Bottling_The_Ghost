Sure! Here’s a list of 20 different types of dinosaurs:  

1. **Tyrannosaurus rex** (T. rex) – Large carnivore with tiny arms.  
2. **Triceratops** – Three-horned herbivore with a frill.  
3. **Velociraptor** – Small, fast carnivore with a sickle-shaped claw.  
4. **Brachiosaurus** – Long-necked herbivore, known for its towering height.  
5. **Stegosaurus** – Herbivore with plates along its back and spiked tail.  
6. **Ankylosaurus** – Heavily armored herbivore with a clubbed tail.  
7. **Spinosaurus** – Carnivore with a sail-like spine on its back.  
8. **Allosaurus** – Large carnivore, slightly smaller than T. rex.  
9. **Diplodocus** – Long-necked and long-tailed herbivore.  
10. **Iguanodon** – Herbivore with thumb spikes.  
11. **Parasaurolophus** – Herbivore with a long, curved crest on its head.  
12. **Pachycephalosaurus** – Herbivore with a thick, domed skull.  
13. **Carnotaurus** – Carnivore with small horns above its eyes.  
14. **Ceratosaurus** – Carnivore with a horn on its nose.  
15. **Gallimimus** – Fast, ostrich-like herbivore.  
16. **Compsognathus** – Small, chicken-sized carnivore.  
17. **Maiasaura** – "Good mother lizard," known for nesting behavior.  
18. **Therizinosaurus** – Herbivore with huge clawed hands.  
19. **Giganotosaurus** – One of the largest carnivores, rivaling T. rex.  
20. **Microraptor** – Small, feathered dinosaur capable of gliding.  

Each of these dinosaurs represents unique traits and adaptations from the Mesozoic Era!
