To unhide hidden tabs (worksheets) in Excel, follow these steps:

1. **Right-Click on Any Sheet Tab**: Right-click on any visible sheet tab at the bottom of the Excel window.
   
2. **Select "Unhide..."**: From the context menu, choose "Unhide...".

3. **Choose the Hidden Sheet**: A dialog box will appear showing a list of all hidden sheets. Select the sheet you want to unhide.

4. **Click "OK"**: Click the "OK" button, and the selected hidden sheet will become visible.

If you have multiple hidden sheets, you will need to repeat these steps for each one, as Excel only allows you to unhide one sheet at a time.

Alternatively, if the "Unhide..." option is greyed out, there may be no hidden sheets, or the workbook might be protected. In such cases, you would need to unprotect the workbook first.
