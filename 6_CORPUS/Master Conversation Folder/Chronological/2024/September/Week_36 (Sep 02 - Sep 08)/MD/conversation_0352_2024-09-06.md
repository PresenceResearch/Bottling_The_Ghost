please create a presentation from the following outline: 1. Introduction (2-3 minutes)
Warm Greeting: Start with a positive comment to set a friendly tone.
Example: "Hello, thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I'm excited to explore how solar energy can benefit your business."
Introduction: Briefly introduce yourself and your company.
Example: "I'm [Your Name] from [Your Company], a leading provider of commercial solar solutions in the Mid-Atlantic region."
Purpose and Agenda: Clearly state the purpose of the call and share the agenda.
Example: "Today, we'll discuss your energy needs, explore if your site is suitable for solar, and determine whether a PPA or purchase option is the best fit for your business."
2. Rapport Building (2-3 minutes)
Personal Connection: Mention any mutual connections or relevant industry news.
Example: "I noticed your company has been expanding its operations. How has that been going?"
Icebreaker Question: "How has your business been adapting to the changing energy landscape?"
3. Exploration and Discovery (10-15 minutes)
Metrics
Energy Usage and Costs:
"Can you share your annual electricity consumption and average monthly costs?"
"How do these costs impact your overall operational budget?"
Follow-up: "Have you noticed any trends or fluctuations in your energy costs over the past few years?"
MEDPICC - Metrics:
"What specific metrics are you looking to improve with a solar solution?"
"How do you measure success in this area?"
Economic Buyer
Decision Makers:
"Who typically makes financial decisions regarding energy projects in your company?"
"Is there anyone else we should involve in this discussion?"
Follow-up: "How do these decision-makers evaluate new energy projects?"
MEDPICC - Economic Buyer:
"Who has final decision-making authority for this project?"
"Who controls the budget for this initiative?"
Decision Criteria
Evaluation Factors:
"What criteria are most important to you when considering solar solutions?"
"How do you evaluate the return on investment for energy projects?"
Follow-up: "Are there any specific financial metrics or KPIs you use to measure success?"
MEDPICC - Decision Criteria:
"What factors will you consider when making a decision?"
"How will you evaluate potential solutions?"
Identify Pain
Current Challenges:
"What challenges are you currently facing with your current energy setup?"
"How are these issues impacting your business operations?"
Follow-up: "Have you considered any other solutions to address these challenges?"
MEDPICC - Identify Pain:
"What are the biggest pain points you're experiencing with your current energy situation?"
"How critical is it for you to address these issues?"
Implementation Process
Project Timeline:
"What does your ideal implementation process look like?"
"Who would be involved in the implementation?"
MEDPICC - Implementation Process:
"Can you walk me through your decision-making process for new energy projects?"
"What are the typical timelines for decisions on projects of this nature?"
Champion
Internal Advocates:
"Is there someone within your organization who is particularly enthusiastic about solar energy?"
Follow-up: "How does this person influence the decision-making process?"
MEDPICC - Champion:
"Who else in your organization is passionate about solving this problem?"
"How can we work with them to ensure a smooth process?"
Competition
Alternative Solutions:
"Are you considering any other energy solutions or providers?"
Follow-up: "What has been your experience with similar solutions in the past?"
MEDPICC - Competition:
"Are there any other solutions you're evaluating alongside solar?"
"What are the pros and cons of these alternatives in your view?"
4. Suitability and Financial Considerations (5-7 minutes)
Site Suitability
Building Assessment:
"Does your building have any restrictions or challenges for solar installation?"
"Have you had any previous assessments or studies done on your site's solar potential?"
Follow-up: "Are there any specific building codes or zoning restrictions we should be aware of?"
Financial Options
PPA vs. Purchase:
"Would you view solar as a capital investment, or would a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) model be more appealing to you?"
PPA Benefits:
"With a PPA, you can lock in a fixed rate for your electricity, ensuring predictable energy costs without any upfront capital investment."
"How important is cost stability and predictability to your business?"
Purchase Benefits:
"If you were to purchase the solar system outright, you could benefit from long-term ownership and potential tax incentives. How does this align with your financial goals?"
"Are there any specific financial incentives or tax benefits you're looking to leverage with a purchase?"
5. Next Steps and Confirmation (3-5 minutes)
Summarize Key Points: Recap the main insights and confirm understanding.
Example: "To summarize, you're interested in exploring solar options, and we've discussed both PPA and purchase models. We'll need to assess your site's suitability and provide a detailed proposal outlining the benefits and costs of each option."
Outline Next Steps:
"Based on our discussion, I'll prepare a preliminary proposal. Can we schedule a follow-up meeting to review it?"
Confirm the date and time for the next meeting.
Example: "Would you be available for a follow-up call next week to discuss the proposal in more detail?"
6. Closing (1-2 minutes)
Thank You: Express appreciation for their time and openness.
Example: "Thank you for your time today. I'm looking forward to helping you explore solar solutions for your business."
Reiterate Commitment: "We'll work together to determine the best path forward, whether it's through a PPA or a purchase option."
Effective Probing Questions
Here are some additional probing questions to help you delve deeper into each area:
Metrics
Energy Usage: "Can you provide more details about your energy consumption patterns? Are there any peak usage times we should be aware of?"
Cost Impact: "How do energy costs affect your budgeting and forecasting processes?"
Economic Buyer
Decision Process: "Can you walk me through the typical decision-making process for energy projects in your company?"
Budget Control: "Who controls the budget for these projects, and what are their key considerations?"
Decision Criteria
Evaluation Metrics: "What specific metrics do you use to evaluate the success of energy projects?"
ROI Expectations: "What return on investment are you expecting from a solar project?"
Identify Pain
Current Challenges: "How are your current energy challenges impacting your operations and bottom line?"
Previous Solutions: "Have you tried any other solutions to address these challenges, and if so, what were the outcomes?"
Champion
Internal Support: "How does the person enthusiastic about solar energy influence the decision-making process?"
Company Strategy: "How does solar energy fit into your company's broader sustainability and energy strategy?"
Site Suitability
Building Restrictions: "Are there any specific building codes or zoning restrictions we should be aware of?"
Previous Assessments: "Have you had any previous solar assessments or studies done on your site? If so, what were the findings?"
Financial Options
PPA Benefits:
"How important is avoiding upfront capital costs to your business?"
"Are there any concerns about the long-term commitment of a PPA?"
Purchase Benefits:
"How do you see the long-term ownership of a solar system aligning with your business goals?"
"Are there any specific tax incentives or financial benefits you're looking to leverage with a purchase?"
