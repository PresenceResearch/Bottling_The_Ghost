Yes, you can duplicate a solar project in Aurora Solar. To duplicate a project, follow these steps:

1. **Log in to Aurora Solar**: Open your Aurora Solar account and go to the dashboard where your projects are listed.

2. **Find the Project**: Locate the solar project you want to duplicate. You can use the search bar or scroll through your list of projects.

3. **Select the Project**: Click on the project name to open it.

4. **Duplicate the Project**:
   - In the project view, look for a menu or options button, usually represented by three dots (ellipsis) or a similar icon.
   - Click on the "Duplicate" or "Clone" option. This may be labeled differently depending on the version or configuration of Aurora Solar you are using.
   - You may be prompted to provide a new name for the duplicated project. Enter the new name and save.

5. **Review the Duplicated Project**: Once duplicated, the new project will appear on your dashboard or project list. Review the new project to ensure all the data has been copied correctly.

Duplicating a project allows you to use existing designs, configurations, and settings as a base for a new project, saving time and effort.
