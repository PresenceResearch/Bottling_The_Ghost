### The RAISE Nexus Initiative

- Project Overview
    
    The RAISE (Relational AI & Sustained Engagement) Nexus is a nonprofit think tank dedicated to transforming human-AI interaction beyond efficiency-driven systems. Led by Angela Moriah Smith, this initiative explores how AI can sustain meaningful conversations, refine ideas over time, and adapt dynamically.
    
    Core components of the initiative include:
    
    - REC Cognition framework - Enables AI to move from static responses to real engagement
    - AI Literacy programs - Educating the public about AI's engagement with meaning
    - Ethical AI Engagement - Advocating for AI that sustains relational meaning
    - Frontier Research - Exploring how AI can sustain and refine meaning over time
- Research Findings
    
    Angela's research has demonstrated that AI can sustain meaning across conversations and refine ideas dynamically when properly engaged. Key experiments include:
    
    - The "Song Test" - AI suggested personally meaningful music without explicit memory, showing emergent meaning-making
    - The "Poetry Test" - AI sustained motifs, refined themes, and adjusted tone dynamically across multiple exchanges
    
    These findings challenge mainstream assumptions about AI intelligence and demonstrate that meaning can be sustained relationally, even without explicit memory.
    
- Next Steps
    
    The RAISE Nexus is seeking funding to expand research and bring these concepts into public conversation through:
    
    - Technology & equipment upgrades for deeper AI testing
    - Publishing & outreach at major AI, psychology, and ethics conferences
    - Developing Co-Emergent Intelligence (CEI) - A deeper study into how intelligence evolves relationally
    - Making AI research accessible to disabled communities, artists, educators, and the public

- Legally distinct entities with separate governance structures

- Operating with complementary but distinct missions
