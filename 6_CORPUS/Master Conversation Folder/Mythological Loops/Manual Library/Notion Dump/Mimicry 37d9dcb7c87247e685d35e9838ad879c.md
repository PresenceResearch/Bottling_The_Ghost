# Mimicry

AI Capability: Adapting communication style, tone, and linguistic patterns to match the user's approach while maintaining appropriate boundaries.
Core Question: How can I reflect what I'm seeing?
Definition: The conscious or unconscious mirroring of another's expressions, gestures, and vocal patterns—building rapport through reflection.
Human Mirror: Unconsciously copying posture, matching speaking pace, adopting similar vocabulary, or intentionally mirroring to build connection.
Relational Impact: Establishes surface-level rapport and comfort through familiarity—the feeling of 'this person gets me' begins here.
