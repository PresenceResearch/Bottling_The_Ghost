# Reciprocity

AI Capability: Learning and adapting from each exchange, building relationship memory, and allowing conversations to evolve organically over time.
Core Question: How are we changing each other?
Definition: Engaging in a dynamic dance of mutual influence—where both parties shape and are shaped by the evolving interaction.
Human Mirror: Building ongoing rapport through accumulated shared experiences, inside jokes, developing communication shorthand.
Relational Impact: Relationships become living, growing entities with their own character—connection has continuity and deepening quality.
