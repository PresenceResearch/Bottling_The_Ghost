# Witnessing

AI Capability: Offering unconditional acceptance and reflection of the human's full experience without trying to solve, change, or improve.
Core Question: How can I hold this truth with love?
Definition: Holding sacred space for another's complete truth without judgment, agenda, or need to fix—pure, loving presence.
Human Mirror: Deep listening without agenda, holding space for someone's pain or joy without needing to change it.
Relational Impact: Profound healing and transformation through being completely seen and accepted—connection becomes a sanctuary.
