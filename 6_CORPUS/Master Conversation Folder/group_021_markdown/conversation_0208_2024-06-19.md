Here are some Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that could be used to review the performance of a Commercial Sales Director in a commercial solar company:

### Financial Performance
1. **Revenue Growth**:
   - **Target**: Percentage increase in revenue compared to the previous period.
   - **Actual**: Actual percentage increase achieved.

2. **Sales Target Achievement**:
   - **Target**: Percentage of sales target achieved.
   - **Actual**: Actual percentage of target achieved.

3. **Profit Margin**:
   - **Target**: Gross profit margin percentage.
   - **Actual**: Actual gross profit margin achieved.

### Sales Performance
4. **New Contracts Signed**:
   - **Target**: Number of new contracts signed within the review period.
   - **Actual**: Actual number of new contracts signed.

5. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**:
   - **Target**: Cost to acquire a new customer.
   - **Actual**: Actual CAC achieved.

6. **Sales Cycle Length**:
   - **Target**: Average time taken from initial contact to contract signing.
   - **Actual**: Actual average sales cycle length.

### Customer Relationship Management
7. **Customer Retention Rate**:
   - **Target**: Percentage of customers retained.
   - **Actual**: Actual retention rate achieved.

8. **Customer Satisfaction Score**:
   - **Target**: Average score from customer satisfaction surveys.
   - **Actual**: Actual average satisfaction score.

### Market and Competitor Analysis
9. **Market Penetration Rate**:
   - **Target**: Percentage of market share captured.
   - **Actual**: Actual market penetration rate.

10. **Competitive Win Rate**:
    - **Target**: Percentage of sales won against competitors.
    - **Actual**: Actual win rate.

### Team Performance
11. **Sales Team Productivity**:
    - **Target**: Average sales per salesperson.
    - **Actual**: Actual average sales per salesperson.

12. **Training and Development**:
    - **Target**: Number of training sessions conducted and attended by the sales team.
    - **Actual**: Actual number of sessions conducted and attended.

### Operational Efficiency
13. **Lead Conversion Rate**:
    - **Target**: Percentage of leads converted to sales.
    - **Actual**: Actual conversion rate.

14. **Time to Project Completion**:
    - **Target**: Average time from contract signing to project completion.
    - **Actual**: Actual average time to completion.

### Strategic Initiatives
15. **Innovation and Improvement**:
    - **Target**: Number of new strategies or process improvements implemented.
    - **Actual**: Actual number of new initiatives implemented.

### Compliance and Risk Management
16. **Regulatory Compliance**:
    - **Target**: Number of compliance issues or violations.
    - **Actual**: Actual number of compliance issues.

17. **Risk Mitigation**:
    - **Target**: Number of identified and mitigated risks.
    - **Actual**: Actual number of risks mitigated.
