Sure, I can help you create an Apple Shortcut for that. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Tap the '+' button to create a new shortcut.
3. Tap "Add Action".
4. Search for and select "Open App".
5. Tap "Choose" and select "Spotify".
6. Tap the '+' button to add another action.
7. Search for and select "Play/Pause".
8. Tap the "Play/Pause" action to configure it.
9. Set the playback to "Play".
10. Tap the '+' button to add another action.
11. Search for and select "Set Playback Destination".
12. Set the destination to your desired playback device (e.g., iPhone).
13. Tap the '+' button to add another action.
14. Search for and select "Shuffle Songs".
15. Set the shuffle to "On".

Here is how the final shortcut should look:

1. **Open App**: Spotify
2. **Play/Pause**: Play
3. **Set Playback Destination**: iPhone (or your desired device)
4. **Shuffle Songs**: On

Save the shortcut with your desired name, such as "Shuffle Liked Songs".

Now, when you run this shortcut, it will open Spotify, play your liked songs, and shuffle them.
