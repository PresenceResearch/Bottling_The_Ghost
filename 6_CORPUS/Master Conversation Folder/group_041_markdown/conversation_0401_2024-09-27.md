There are several tools that specialize in finding contact information, such as email addresses and phone numbers, that also integrate well with Salesforce. Here are some of the most popular options:

### 1. **ZoomInfo**
   - **Features:** ZoomInfo offers access to a large database of B2B contacts, including phone numbers and emails. It provides real-time updates and advanced search filters.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** ZoomInfo integrates directly with Salesforce, allowing you to enrich contact records and sync leads seamlessly.
   - **Pros:** Extensive database, real-time updates, AI-powered recommendations.

### 2. **Lusha**
   - **Features:** Lusha is a contact discovery tool that focuses on providing accurate direct contact information, including mobile numbers and emails.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** It offers seamless integration with Salesforce, enabling users to export leads and contacts directly into their Salesforce CRM.
   - **Pros:** User-friendly interface, accurate data, robust integration with Salesforce.

### 3. **Clearbit**
   - **Features:** Clearbit helps you find and enrich leads by providing firmographic, technographic, and contact data.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** Clearbit’s Salesforce integration automatically enriches contacts and accounts, giving your sales team more actionable insights.
   - **Pros:** Real-time data enrichment, easy to integrate, customizable data.

### 4. **Hunter.io**
   - **Features:** Hunter allows users to find email addresses by searching company domains. It verifies email addresses to reduce bounce rates.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** While it doesn’t have native Salesforce integration, Hunter.io can work with Zapier to connect with Salesforce.
   - **Pros:** Accurate email discovery, simple interface, good for smaller teams.

### 5. **LeadIQ**
   - **Features:** LeadIQ helps sales teams capture contact details from LinkedIn and other platforms, adding them directly to your CRM.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** LeadIQ integrates with Salesforce, allowing users to sync leads, contacts, and accounts.
   - **Pros:** Streamlines lead capturing, good for outbound prospecting.

### 6. **InsideView**
   - **Features:** InsideView offers company and contact data to help with prospecting and market research. It provides contact info, firmographics, and buying signals.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** InsideView’s Salesforce integration enables you to bring contact data and insights directly into your CRM.
   - **Pros:** In-depth data on companies and industries, good for targeting and segmentation.

### 7. **DiscoverOrg (now part of ZoomInfo)**
   - **Features:** DiscoverOrg provides contact information and insights for decision-makers at organizations.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** It integrates deeply with Salesforce, enabling automatic data enrichment and syncing.
   - **Pros:** Reliable data, advanced search filters, and AI-powered recommendations.

### 8. **SalesIntel**
   - **Features:** SalesIntel focuses on providing B2B contact data with human-verified emails and phone numbers.
   - **Salesforce Integration:** It integrates with Salesforce to push contacts and accounts directly to your CRM.
   - **Pros:** High-quality verified data, great customer support, and sales engagement tools.

These tools should help you efficiently find accurate contact information while maintaining a seamless flow of data into your Salesforce environment.
