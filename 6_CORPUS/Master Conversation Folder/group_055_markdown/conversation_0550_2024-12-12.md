If you enjoyed *Step Brothers* and its mix of absurd humor, sibling rivalry, and immature antics, here are some similar movies that you might like:

1. **Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby** (2006)  
   Another hilarious Will Ferrell and John C. Reilly collaboration, this movie is about a NASCAR driver and his over-the-top lifestyle and rivalries.

2. **The Other Guys** (2010)  
   Will Ferrell teams up with Mark Wahlberg in this buddy cop comedy filled with ridiculous antics and quotable one-liners.

3. **Dumb and Dumber** (1994)  
   Jim Carrey and Jeff Daniels play two loveable idiots who embark on a road trip full of hilarious misadventures.

4. **Superbad** (2007)  
   A coming-of-age comedy about two best friends trying to have one epic night before graduation, filled with awkward situations and raunchy humor.

5. **Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy** (2004)  
   Another classic Will Ferrell comedy, focusing on a clueless 1970s news anchor and his ridiculous antics.

6. **Tropic Thunder** (2008)  
   A satirical action-comedy about a group of actors who find themselves in a real war situation while filming a movie.

7. **Napoleon Dynamite** (2004)  
   While slightly more quirky and awkward, this comedy centers around a lovable weirdo navigating small-town life.

8. **Role Models** (2008)  
   Starring Paul Rudd and Seann William Scott, this movie follows two guys forced to mentor kids as part of a community service sentence, leading to plenty of laugh-out-loud moments.

9. **Pineapple Express** (2008)  
   A stoner comedy about a friendship between a process server and his weed dealer who get caught up in a wild adventure.

10. **Hot Rod** (2007)  
    Andy Samberg plays an amateur stuntman trying to raise money for his stepfather's surgery by performing a big jump, all while dealing with his dysfunctional family.

11. **This Is the End** (2013)  
    A comedy about a group of celebrities (playing exaggerated versions of themselves) who must survive the apocalypse while stuck in James Franco's house.

12. **Old School** (2003)  
    Will Ferrell, Vince Vaughn, and Luke Wilson star in this movie about three men who start a college fraternity to relive their glory days.

All these movies share the same kind of over-the-top humor, ridiculous scenarios, and sometimes heartwarming moments that made *Step Brothers* so memorable.
