To pair your Verizon E3200 Wi-Fi Extender with your Verizon Internet Gateway, follow these steps:

**Wireless (WPS) Pairing:**

1. **Position the Extender:** Plug the E3200 into a power outlet near your Verizon Internet Gateway. Wait for the Status LED to turn solid yellow.

2. **Initiate Pairing:** Press and hold the Unified button (located on the front of the extender) for 3 seconds. Within 2 minutes, press the pair button on your Verizon Internet Gateway. Both devices' LEDs should blink blue, indicating pairing mode.

3. **Confirm Pairing:** Once the extender's LED shows a solid white or blinking yellow light, the pairing is successful.

4. **Relocate the Extender:** Unplug the extender and move it to a location between the gateway and the area with weak Wi-Fi coverage. Plug it into a power outlet.

5. **Optimize Placement:**
   - If the LED blinks yellow rapidly, move the extender further from the gateway.
   - If it blinks yellow slowly, move it closer.
   - A solid white LED indicates optimal placement.

**Wired (Ethernet) Pairing:**

1. **Connect via Ethernet:** Use an Ethernet cable to connect the E3200 to your Verizon Internet Gateway.

2. **Power On:** Plug the extender into a power outlet and wait for the LED to turn solid yellow.

3. **Confirm Pairing:** When the LED turns solid white or blinks yellow, pairing is complete.

4. **Optimize Placement:**
   - If the LED blinks yellow rapidly, reposition the extender further from the gateway.
   - If it blinks yellow slowly, move it closer.
   - A solid white LED signifies proper placement.
