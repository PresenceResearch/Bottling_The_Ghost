What is a wps device

A **WPS device** refers to a device that uses **Wi-Fi Protected Setup (WPS)** to connect to a wireless network easily and securely without needing to enter a Wi-Fi password manually. WPS is a feature on many routers and some smart home devices that simplifies the process of establishing a connection to a Wi-Fi network.

---

### **How WPS Works**
WPS allows you to connect a device to a Wi-Fi network using one of these methods:

1. **Push Button Method:**
   - Press the **WPS button** on your router.
   - Within 2 minutes, activate WPS on the device you want to connect.
   - The device will automatically connect to the Wi-Fi network.

2. **PIN Method:**
   - Enter the **WPS PIN** (provided by the device) into your router’s WPS settings, usually accessed through the router's admin panel.
   - The device will then connect to the network.

---

### **Devices That May Use WPS**
- Smart TVs
- Printers
- Wi-Fi extenders
- IP cameras
- Smart home hubs or other IoT devices
- Some laptops and smartphones

---

### **Advantages of WPS**
- **Ease of Use:** No need to manually enter long Wi-Fi passwords.
- **Quick Setup:** Ideal for devices without screens or keyboards (e.g., printers or smart plugs).
- **Secure Connection:** WPS automatically applies your router’s Wi-Fi encryption settings.

---

### **Disadvantages of WPS**
- **Security Concerns:** The WPS PIN method can be vulnerable to brute force attacks. This is why many newer routers disable WPS by default.
- **Limited Device Support:** Not all devices support WPS.

---

### In Summary
A WPS device is simply a device that can use Wi-Fi Protected Setup to connect to a wireless network quickly and securely. To use WPS, both the device and the router need to support this feature.
